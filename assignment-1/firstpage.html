Location of School

Arifiye Science High School is in city of Sakarya in Turkey. It located in Arifiye county which is one of the main counties of Sakarya. Also it is very close to Sakarya-Istanbul highway and train station.

Information About Schoolyard

Inside of the School Building

The school building has 5 floors. There are 12 classes inside of the school. There is a cafeteria, conference hall and school managers room in first floor. Computer class and mosque are in second floor. Classes are in third,fourth and fifth floors.

A class contains 30 benchs. Classes are modern and large so they are spacious. Classes contain smart board, benchs and desk.
