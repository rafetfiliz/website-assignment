History of Arifiye Science High School

Main Page- General Information- History- Academic Staff- About Me

Our school started to giving education with name of Arifiye Instructor Training Course in 1937. Our school's founder director is Süleyman Edip Balkır. After 1940 it's name has changed as Arifiye Village Institute and Instructor Training Course.Until 1953 our school trained people under this name. After 1953 Village Institutes are closed and our school's name has changed as Arifiye First Teacher School and gave education until 1975 with this name.Between 1977 and 1979 name of school has changed again and gave education with name of Arifiye Education Institute and Teacher High School.

In 1979 school's name has changed once again. Between 1979 and 1990 our school gave education with name of Arifiye Teacher High School. In 1990 our school started to giving English lessons and it's name has changed as Arifiye Anatolian Teacher High School.In 2014 Teacher Schools are closed and our school named as Arifiye Science High School.

In every summer some of our graduated stundents come to school together for commemorate their times in school. Also a famous Turkish folk music singer Aşık Veysel gave music education in our school in 1942.Also second president of Turkey İsmet İnönü visited our school in 1938.

There is a list of our school's names in chronological order:

  1. Arifiye Instructor Training Course
  2. Arifiye Village Institute and Instructor Training Course
  3. Arifiye First Teacher School
  4. Arifiye Education Institute and Teacher High School
  5. Arifiye Teacher High School
  6. Arifiye Anatolian Teacher High School
  7. Arifiye Science High School
